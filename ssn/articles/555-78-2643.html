In the 1979 M*A*S*H TV series episode 'The Young and the Restless', first aired on January 22, 1979, Corporal Klinger changes his section 8 ploy from cross-dressing to pretending he is a civilian in Toledo and not in the army. During his "exit interview" with Colonel Potter, he reveals his Social Security number as 555-78-2643. In more recent airings, the center portion of the number is usually muted or bleeped.
