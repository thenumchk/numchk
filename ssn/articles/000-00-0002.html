In the Simpsons animated TV Series, Season 11, Episode 12: The Mansion Family first aired January 23, 2000: character Montgomery Burns, voiced by Harry Shearer, reveals Mr. Burns social security number as "nought-nought-nought, nought-nought, nought-nought-nought-two" as he writes it on his applications form. The use of the british for of zero is excellent character development. Any form of british ling could have been used for the same effect: Nought, naught, ought or aught.
